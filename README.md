## **3. PESTL Analysis**

| Factor | Cape Town | Pretoria |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| **Political** | Stable local government, pro-business incentives | Proximity to national government offices, political influence |
| **Economic** | Strong tourism, high disposable income | Higher corporate spending, lower tourism impact |
| **Social** | Diverse population, high expatriate community | Young professionals, families, commuter-heavy |
| **Technological** | Growing adoption of online car platforms | Increasing digital services, fleet management tech |
| **Legal** | Standard automotive regulations | Vehicle import/export compliance focus |
| **Environmental** | Coastal environmental regulations | Urban emissions and fleet standards |

## **5. Location Comparison â€“ Cape Town vs Pretoria**
